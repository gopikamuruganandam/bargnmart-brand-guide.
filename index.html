🛍️ Barg'N-Mart

Brand Persona & Voice Guide

1. Brand Overview

Barg'N-Mart is a fictional discount superstore chain offering groceries, clothes, home goods, and electronics — all under one roof, at unbeatable prices.

Brand Metaphor: "Like that savvy aunt who knows all the best deals and wants you to win too."

👤 2. Brand Persona

  • Friendly: Like a helpful neighbor.
  • Humorous: Light-hearted and pun-loving.
  • Value-driven: Focused on saving customers money.
  • Loyal: Cares deeply about everyday people.
  • Reliable: Honest service

🎯 3. Target Audience

  • Primary: Budget-conscious families, students, seniors, and working parents.
  • Income: Low to middle-income groups.
  • Location: Urban and semi-urban areas, small towns.
  • Behavior: Price-sensitive, value-over-brand buyers.
  • Need: Feel smart and confident saving money without compromising on quality.

🎤 4. Brand Voice & Tone

  • ✨ Warm & Cheerful: Makes customers feel welcome and appreciated.
  • 🎯 Clear & Direct: Straight to the point and easy to understand.
  • 😄 Fun but Focused: Adds a sprinkle of fun without distraction.
  • 💪 Empowering: Helps customers feel like smart shoppers.

✅ 5. Do’s and Don’ts

✅ Do’s (The Barg'N Way!)

  • ✨ Speak like you're helping a friend — warm, casual, and clear.
  • 🎯 Keep it real — honest savings beat fancy talk every time.
  • 😄 Add a little fun — puns, rhymes, and good vibes welcome.
  • 📱 Use emojis, especially on social — they add personality!
  • 🛍️ Make CTAs snappy — like “Snag it now!”
  • 💡 Talk benefits, not buzzwords.
  • 👏 Celebrate the customer — they’re the savings hero!

❌ Don’ts (The Barg'N Way!)

  • 🤖 Don’t sound like a robot — we’re human here.
  • 📚 Skip corporate mumbo jumbo.
  • 🚀 Don’t overpromise — real savings win.
  • 🙃 No sarcasm — keep it kind.
  • 💤 Avoid boring CTAs — “Learn more” won’t cut it.
  • 🧊 Don’t sound cold — be warm and real.
  • 🎭 Be authentic — Barg'N-Mart has its own charm.
